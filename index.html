About

Healthy, wholesome living — backed by expertise.

I'm a former Hong Kong national-team fencer — ranked No. 1 in the junior division and top 3 in junior Asia — now working full time as a sports physiotherapist for soccer and rugby teams, with an orthopaedic specialty.

That blend of athletic discipline and clinical expertise shapes everything I make. But beyond the science, my feed is a warm, wholesome corner of the internet — everyday lifestyle, healthy routines, and longevity habits that help my audience feel confident, balanced, and genuinely well.
